Providing information and guidelines for government procurement projects.

Accreditation@SG

Accreditation@SG is a programme to accelerate the growth of Singapore-based innovative technology product companies through winning contracts with government agencies and large enterprises, as well as facilitating new growth capital into the companies.

GeBIZ

GeBIZ is the Singapore Government's one-stop e-procurement portal for any supplier to access the public sector business opportunities.

Open Innovation Platform (OIP)

Open Innovation Platform (OIP) by IMDA seeks to accelerate digital innovation by matching real business challenges of Problem Owners to Tech Solution providers.
