COVID-19 Chatbot for businesses

This chatbot was created to aid employers to stay updated on the news following the COVID-19 virus.

It aims to provide answers based on the programmed questions on the existing situation and employers are able to find out more information such as the help that is currently available for them and how to ensure that their business continue in this trying times.

This bot was created by the Ministy of Trade and Industry (MTI) and the Government Technology Agency (GovTech). Chatbots are seen as a common feature used by most government agencies in Singapore. An example is the Ask Jamie Chatbot which is also developed by GovTech which is a virtual assistant designed to answer queries within specific domains on Government agency websites.

Businesses can access the chatbot here https://www.go.gov.sg/businessconnect.
