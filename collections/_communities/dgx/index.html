DIGITAL GOVERNMENT EXCHANGE

Organised by the Smart Nation and Digital Government Group Singapore, the Digital Government Exchange (DGX) conducted its inaugural conference in 2016.

DGX is a two-day invitation-only event for digital government leaders to engage with like minded agencies to transform and develop better digital services. The annual event facilitates insightful conversations on the impact of technologies on communities and economies. Members include Australia, Canada, China, Estonia, Finland, Israel, Japan, New Zealand, Sweden, The Netherlands, UK and US and multilateral organisation representatives from the United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs and World Bank.

In addition to the annual conference, DGX has convened special webinars in response to the crisis to discuss pertinent topics including contact tracing and most recently, safe travel.

Digital Government Exchange - Safe Travel Working Group

Globally, there are ongoing discussions on vaccine certificates could be part of the effort to reopen the global economy. Members recognised the increasing need to collaborate in preparation for cross border verification. A special edition DGX webinar was conducted on 16 March 2021.

DGX members agreed that technical discussions for cross border recognition of digital health certificates should ommence to ensure member countries were ready when policy discussions were completed. To continue the discussions, the DGX Safe Travel Working Group was formed. Members include Australia, Canada, Israel, Japan, New Zealand, Sweden, the UK and UNDESA. The first working group meeting was held on 15 April 2021.
